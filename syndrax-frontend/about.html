Syndrax helps multi-channel sellers run pricing, inventory, and orders — with account health treated as a first-class system, not a bolt-on.

What Syndrax is

A compliance-first marketplace automation platform for resellers on eBay and expanding channels. Listings, pricing, inventory sync, and order visibility — with real-time risk signals so restrictions are caught early.

What we do with an eBay connection

When you connect eBay, Syndrax requests access needed to read seller performance, account limits, inventory, and order status through official APIs. That data powers AXIS risk surfaces. Syndrax does not create, modify, or delete listings without your explicit action.

  • Seller performance — standing, defects, shipping, policy signals
  • Account information — privileges and limits
  • Inventory — active listing / stock status
  • Orders — open orders and fulfillment status

Account safety

  • OAuth 2.0 — your eBay password is never shared with Syndrax
  • Tokens encrypted and refreshed; they expire
  • You can revoke access anytime in eBay account settings
  • Official APIs only — no hostile scraping bots

Company

Syndrax LLC is a US-based software company building marketplace automation tools for operators who need speed without burning accounts.
